# User Personas

## Primary School Student
A young learner who needs simple explanations, visual support, and structured practice.

## Middle School Student
A student who needs help with foundations, revision, and habit building.

## High School Student
A student preparing for exams who needs summaries, quizzes, and organized study spaces.

## University Student
A learner managing multiple courses, lecture notes, PDFs, and assignments.

## Professional Learner
An adult learning technical skills, certifications, or career development topics.

## Teacher
An educator who wants to publish learning material, assign tasks, and guide students.

## Institution Admin
A school, university, or academy administrator managing groups and learning programs.
