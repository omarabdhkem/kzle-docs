# Product Philosophy

Kzle is designed around the idea that learning should feel organized, personal, and intelligent.

## Principles

### AI First
AI should actively reduce repetitive work and help learners understand content faster.

### User-Owned Workspace
Each learner owns a flexible workspace for any subject, course, or goal.

### Connected Learning
Files, summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and conversations should all stay linked.

### Accessible Everywhere
The platform should sync across devices and feel consistent on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

### Built for Growth
The architecture should support expansion into community, teachers, courses, and marketplace features.
